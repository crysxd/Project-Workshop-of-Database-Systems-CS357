Order your meal online in a few steps!

1. Select your favorite food online

Search on my-burger.com for your favorite restaurant and select your meal and drinks.

2. Login using your mobile number

Login simply using your mobile number and your name. No online payment information are required as you pay on arrival.

3. Wait for the courier to deliver your food

After you placed your order you will be informed about the ETA via SMS. As soon as the courier is on his way to you, you will receive a second SMS.

4. Pay on arrival and enjoy your meal!

The courier will call you as soon as he is on your address. Attend him, receive your food and pay him directly

Enjoy your meal!
